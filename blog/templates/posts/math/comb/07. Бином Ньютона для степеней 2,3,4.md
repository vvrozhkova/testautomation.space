### –¢–µ–æ—Ä–µ–º–∞

$(x+y)^n = {C}_{n}^{0} x^n y^0 + {C}_{n}^{1} x^{n-1} y^1 + {C}_{n}^{2} x^{n-2} y^2 + ... + {C}_{n}^{n-1} x^{1} y^{n-1} + {C}_{n}^{n} x^{0} y^{n} =

$\frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!} = \frac{n!}{(n-k)!(n-n+k)!}$

$\frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!} = \frac{n!}{(n-k)!k!}$
